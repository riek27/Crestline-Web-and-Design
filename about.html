About Crestline

We are a passionate team of web developers and designers based in Juba, dedicated to creating exceptional digital experiences for businesses across South Sudan and beyond.

Our Story

Crestline Web & Design was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to bring world-class web development to businesses in South Sudan. Our founders recognized a growing need for high-quality digital solutions in the region and set out to create an agency that could compete with international standards while understanding local markets.

Today, we've grown into a team of dedicated professionals who have delivered over 10 successful projects for clients across various industries. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted partner for businesses looking to establish or enhance their online presence.

Our Mission

To empower businesses in South Sudan with premium web solutions that enhance their digital presence, drive growth, and create lasting impact in the global marketplace.

We believe every business deserves a website that reflects their unique value proposition and connects them with their target audience effectively.

Our Vision

To become the leading web development agency in East Africa, recognized for our innovation, quality, and commitment to client success.

We aim to bridge the digital divide by making premium web development accessible to businesses of all sizes across the region.
