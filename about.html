Our Story: Pioneering Digital Excellence in South Sudan

Crestline Web & Design was founded in 2018 in the heart of Juba, South Sudan, with a vision to transform the digital landscape of our nation. Our founders recognized a significant gap in the market: while businesses in South Sudan were eager to establish an online presence, they lacked access to premium, professional web development services that could compete on a global scale.

What began as a small team of two passionate developers has grown into Juba's premier web development agency, serving clients across various industries including hospitality, retail, NGOs, government agencies, and startups. We've successfully delivered over 150+ websites and digital solutions that have helped South Sudanese businesses expand their reach, increase revenue, and establish authority in their respective markets.

Our journey has been guided by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and local understanding. We combine international web standards with deep insights into the South Sudanese market, creating digital solutions that are not only visually stunning but also culturally relevant and commercially effective.
